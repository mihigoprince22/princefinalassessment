Our Mission

At Mihigo Management and Co., our mission is to redefine the real estate experience in Rwanda by providing unparalleled service, expert guidance, and a diverse portfolio of properties tailored to the unique needs of our clients. We are dedicated to facilitating seamless transactions, fostering long-term relationships, and contributing to the sustainable growth and development of Rwanda's real estate sector.

Our Founder

Mihigo Prince

Mihigo Prince is the visionary founder of Mihigo Management and Co., bringing a wealth of experience and expertise to the real estate industry in Rwanda. With a background in project management and a keen eye for innovation, Mihigo has played a pivotal role in shaping the company's direction. Graduating with a Bachelor of Arts in Project Management from Kepler College, Mihigo honed his skills in problem-solving and strategic planning, essential qualities for navigating the complexities of the real estate market. His hands-on experience in real estate transactions, coupled with his commitment to excellence, has earned him a reputation for delivering exceptional results. Throughout his career, Mihigo has demonstrated a strong aptitude for customer service and a deep understanding of market dynamics. His dedication to providing tailored solutions and fostering long-term relationships has been instrumental in establishing Mihigo Management and Co. as a trusted name in Rwanda's real estate sector. Outside of his professional endeavors, Mihigo is passionate about environmental sustainability and community development. He believes in the importance of giving back to society and actively participates in initiatives aimed at creating positive change in his community. Mihigo's leadership, coupled with his entrepreneurial spirit and commitment to integrity, continues to drive Mihigo Management and Co. towards new heights of success in Rwanda's dynamic real estate landscape.
